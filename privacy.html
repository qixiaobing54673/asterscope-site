Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-24

AsterScope ("the app") is developed by Zhuan Zhang.

No Account Required

AsterScope does not require you to create an account or provide any personal identifier. All functionality is available without sign-in.

Data We Use On Your Device

The following data is processed on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers (we do not operate servers):

  • Location (when you grant permission): used to compute your Tonight Plan score, rank nearby dark-sky sites by distance, and calculate rise/set times for celestial objects.
  • Motion and Orientation (when you use AR features): used to align the interactive sky chart with the physical sky.
  • Observation Logbook Entries: your session notes, equipment records, and target history. Stored locally in iOS user defaults and the app sandbox. Not synced to iCloud unless you explicitly enable iCloud Documents.

Third-Party Services

AsterScope fetches the following data from third parties. None of these services receive any personal identifier from us.

  • Apple WeatherKit — weather forecasts. Apple's privacy policy applies.
  • NASA GIBS — public light pollution imagery tiles. No personal data sent.
  • 7Timer ASTRO — public astronomical site forecasts. Only your coarse latitude/longitude is sent.
  • The Sky Live and COBS — public comet data. No personal data sent.
  • Space.com, EarthSky, In-The-Sky, Astronomy.com (News tab) — public RSS feeds. No personal data sent.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Email addresses
  • Names
  • Contact lists
  • Photos (unless you explicitly attach one to a logbook entry)
  • Payment information (handled by Apple StoreKit)
  • Analytics, crash reports, or any telemetry via third-party SDKs

In-App Purchases

AsterScope offers a one-time lifetime purchase via Apple StoreKit. Apple processes payment data. We do not see or store your payment details.

Children

AsterScope is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children. No data is collected from any user of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the effective date above will be updated. Material changes will also be noted in the app's version history.
